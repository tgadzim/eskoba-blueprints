Lesson 1: What Is Andromeda2026?

Andromeda2026 is Eskoba's Meta Advertising operating system. It standardizes how campaigns are launched, tested, validated and scaled.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the purpose of Andromeda2026
  • Understand the framework components
  • Understand the decision-making process

Why Most Marketers Fail

Most marketers make decisions based on feelings. Andromeda2026 forces decisions to be made using data.
